Artificial Intelligence

  • What is Artficial Intelligence?

    • Artificial intelligence (AI) is the science and engineering of making machines that mimic human problem-solving and decision-making capabilities. AI uses computer science, mathematical logic, statistics, probabilities, computational neurobiology, and other sciences and theories to achieve this goal. AI enables machines to learn from experience, adjust to new inputs, and perform human-like tasks.

  • What are the types of AI?

  1. Reactive Machines
  2. Limited Memory Machines
  3. Theory of Mind Machines
  4. Self-Aware Machines
